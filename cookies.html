Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and understand how you interact with it.

2. How We Use Cookies

Akeef Studio uses minimal cookies to:

  • Essential cookies: Ensure the website functions correctly (e.g., form submissions).
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website so we can improve the experience. These collect anonymous data such as pages visited and time on site.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies, including:

  • Google Analytics — for website usage statistics
  • Web3Forms — for processing contact form submissions

These services have their own privacy and cookie policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored
  • Delete individual or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of this website.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
